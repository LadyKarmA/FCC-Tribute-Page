Elvis Presely
1935-1977
December 21, 1970 -The day the King met with the President

1935 - Elvis Aron Presley was born in Tupelo, Mississippi to parents Vernon and Gladys. His twin brother, Jesse Garon Presley, was stillborn 35 minutes prior.
1945 - Elvis' first public apperance - A talent contest at a Fair and Dairy show. He also recieved his first guitar as a 10th birthday this year.
1947 - Presley's first on air preformace on the radio station WELO.
1958 - Drafted to the US Army. It was during his 2 years of service with 3rd Armored Division in Friedberg, Germany that he met his eventual wife Priscilla.
